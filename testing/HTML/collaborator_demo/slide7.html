Collaborator & Conductor

GAUDY has two killer features; bright colours, and friendship!

Conductor

Gaudy has two modes of operation. The first is the conductor. It is responsible for connecting to websites and downloading content, navigating through the history, and managing the collaborators.

Collaborator

The collaborator mode connects to a conductor, and when the conductor visits a page, the collaborator also views it. Friends or colleagues can share a web browsing session while limiting access to just viewing the page.


You can see the conductor and collaborator side by side now. Notice the pretty buttons in the conductor.
